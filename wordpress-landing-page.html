Open your browser and go to www.manupgodsway.org/app on your phone or tablet.

2

Tap the share icon (iOS) or the menu icon (Android) and select "Add to Home Screen." The app icon will appear just like any other app.

3

Start Your Journey

Sign in, complete your profile, and begin your first Bible study. Your free trial starts immediately — no credit card required.

📱 iPhone & iPad (iOS Safari)

Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari, scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then tap Add in the top right.

🤖 Android (Chrome)

Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, tap Add to Home Screen (or "Install App" if prompted automatically), then tap Install.
