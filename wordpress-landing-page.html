Three Steps to Get Started

Man Up God's Way is a Progressive Web App. It installs directly from your browser — on any phone or tablet.

1

Visit the App

Open your phone's browser and go to manupgodsway.org/app — no download required.

2

Add to Home Screen

Tap the share or menu button in your browser and choose "Add to Home Screen". The app icon will appear on your device just like a native app.

3

Man Up. Get to Work.

Create your account, start your free trial, and begin your first study. Brotherhood and accountability are waiting.

On iPhone (Safari)

Open Safari → tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen → scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap "Add" in the top right.

On Android (Chrome)

Open Chrome → tap the three-dot menu in the top right → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap "Add" when prompted.
